Work With Me

I work with organisations, teams, and communities to strengthen communication, mental health, and inclusion, by co-creating the conditions where people can learn, connect, and communicate together.

What I offer

Training & workforce development

Workshops, facilitation, and learning programmes focused on communication, mental health, inclusion, and co-produced practice.

Service design & consultancy

Supporting teams and organisations to design or develop services, pathways, and roles that improve outcomes for children and young people.

Bespoke projects & commissioned work

Targeted, commissioned support for service improvement, research, evaluation, community engagement, or system development work.

Communities of practice & learning

Designing and facilitating spaces where professionals, families, and young people learn from each other and build shared approaches to communication and inclusion.

Who I work with

  • Health and mental health services
  • Education and inclusion teams
  • Local, national, and international system leaders
  • Multidisciplinary professional teams
  • Community and third-sector organisations

Approach & Fees

My work is collaborative, co-produced, and grounded in lived experience. Fees are agreed depending on scope, complexity, and duration of work. For exploratory or partnership-based work, commissioning or funding arrangements can be discussed.

Communication. Connection. Belonging.

I work with children, young people, families, professionals, communities and organisations to strengthen communication, mental health, and a sense of belonging, by creating the conditions where people can learn, contribute, and grow together.

At the heart of my work is a simple belief: When people feel understood and connected, they are better able to shape their own lives and support one another.

I’m a Speech & Language Therapist and Cognitive Behavioural Psychotherapist, working across clinical practice, communities, leadership, and service development. I also design and facilitate spaces where people come together to co-create solutions, from local systems development to international collaboration.
